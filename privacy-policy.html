Privacy Policy for SocialSync

Last updated: January 2024

SocialSync ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SocialSync platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including via the Pinterest API and other social media integrations.

Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts (including Pinterest, Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and TikTok), we may access your profile information, posts, and content with your explicit authorization. We do not collect or store your social media login credentials.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to help you:

  • Schedule and publish posts across multiple platforms
  • Manage your social media content
  • Provide analytics and insights
  • Improve our services

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Access via Social Media APIs

SocialSync uses various social media APIs (including Pinterest API) in accordance with each platform's Developer Guidelines and Terms of Service. You must explicitly authorize the actions performed on your social media accounts. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your social media accounts at any time via your account settings or via SocialSync
  • Export your data

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with third-party social media platforms. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

Email: support@socialsync.app

Compliance

SocialSync is committed to complying with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA where applicable.
